Wait, who are you?

A creative who codes. A coder who creates. A writer. A lively problem-solver with logic bending analytical skills and gorgeous design thinking. Leonard Souza is a life-long learner helplessly drawn to the sublime space where design, technology and narrative intersect. He’s written a fun tale, hosts a writing podcast, builds killer software, and teaches UX, design and coding. His words reach 30,000+ people a month.

Leonard has had the pleasure of working with a notable roster of Fortune 500 clients including Intel, Boeing, FedEx, Adobe, Bank of America and more.

When not bashing his head against some ridiculous design challenge, Leonard enjoys literature, hiking, body-hacking, and spending time with his wife and two children.
